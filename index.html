FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Find answers to the most common questions.

How long does approval take?
Most first-time applicants receive an approval decision within 30 minutes. Returning users who applied within the past 30 days are approved instantly.
Is my information safe?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We only use your information to evaluate your application.
Do you charge hidden fees?
No. All fees and rates are transparent and shown to you before you accept a loan.
What are the eligibility requirements?
You must be 18+, have a valid ID and an active bank account. We accept multiple income sources including self-employment.
